About SpeedLink Internet Services

SpeedLink Internet Services provides internet support for home users, students, local shops and small offices. This website is designed as a normal ISP business website with customer support features added into it.

The main purpose of the system is to make complaint handling clearer and more organised. Customers can submit issues online, receive a reference number and track progress without visiting the office repeatedly.
